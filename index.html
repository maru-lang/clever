📝 Example Story

One day, a clever cat found a map to a hidden treasure. She walked until she was about to arrive at the spot. At the bottom of a big tree, there was a hole. Suddenly, an angry bird tried to attack her! The cat was very afraid and quickly tried to hide in the bushes.

The cat realized it was cruel to fight the bird. The bird was just protecting its nest and misunderstood what the cat wanted. Finally, the cat found a shiny coin in her pocket. She threw it on the ground to distract the bird. The bird liked the shiny coin. They seemed to agree to share the space. The cat got her treasure, and the bird kept its nest safe!
